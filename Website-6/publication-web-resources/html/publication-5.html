How it works?

Book your mystery trip.

Prepare with subtle hints through the app.

Board your private carriage.

Choose solitude or connection.

Discover your destination.

Before your journey begins, you choose how you’d like to experience it — in solitude or in connection.

Those who select Solo are placed in quieter carriages designed for

reflection and comfort, among

others who value privacy and calm.

Those who choose Connection travel alongside open, curious

travelers. If you meet someone during the journey and add them on the app, it begins to suggest shared hotels and personalized itineraries based on your com-bined interests — allowing your mystery to continue together, if you both wish.

Whether you travel inward or outward, the experience remains entirely your own.
