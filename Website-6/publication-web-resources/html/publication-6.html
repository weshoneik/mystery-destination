How it works?

Book your mystery trip.

Prepare with subtle hints through the app.

Board your private carriage.

Choose solitude or connection.

Discover your destination.

As the train slows and the landscape begins to change, your destination is finally revealed. The app quietly unlocks — showing where you’ve arrived and suggesting experiences that match your interests. If you’ve connected with another traveler, it offers shared recommendations: places to stay, things to do, and paths to explore together.

Stepping off the train, you enter a new place with open curiosity — not just to see it, but to experience it fully, guided by discovery rather than expectation. The mystery ends, but the journey continues.
