How it works?

Book your mystery trip.

Prepare with subtle hints through the app.

Board your private carriage.

Choose solitude or connection.

Discover your destination.

When you board the sleeper train, you’ll be guided to a carriage that matches your chosen journey style.

If you’ve chosen Solo, you’ll share a quiet space with others who also value reflection and privacy — minimal interaction, soft lighting, and comfort de-signed for rest and calm.

If you’ve chosen Connection, you’ll be placed with travelers open to shared moments and conversation — a warmer, more social environment where curi-osity and community meet.

Every traveler is on their own mystery journey, but together, you all move to-ward the unknown.
